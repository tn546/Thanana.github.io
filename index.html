Summary of the research

Actually, my goal is Astronaut, however, currently, ℳy scientific research concerns with seeking a control framework with emphasis on the model-free and model-based control for the non-linear control problem subjected to both Natural and Artificial systems, i.e., robotics, manufacturing, rehabilitation inspired by biology. To aim at a framework of adaptive control with robustness, the unification of control theory and learning based on optimization algorithms, where the speed of solution is considered Convex/non-convex problem, is on track of the research.
