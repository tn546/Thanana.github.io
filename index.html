Summary of the research

Motivated by learning system of Human, ℳy scientific research concerns with seeking a control framework with emphasis on the model-free control and model-based control to address a non-linear control problem subjected to both Natural and Artificial systems, i.e., robotics, manufacturing, rehabilitation inspired by biology. To aim at the framework of adaptive control with robustness, the unification of control theory and learning based on optimization algorithms, where the speed of solution is considered Convex/non-convex problem, is on track of the research.
